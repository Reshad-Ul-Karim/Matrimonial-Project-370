Privacy Policy

At Matrimonial Hub, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you create a profile on Matrimonial Hub, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name (First Name and Last Name)
  • Date of Birth
  • Religion
  • Ethnicity
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Profile Photo
  • Any other details you voluntarily provide in your profile

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used to help you connect with potential matches and to provide the best user experience possible. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Display your profile to other users of the platform
  • Allow you to search for and connect with potential partners
  • Communicate with you regarding your account and services
  • Improve our platform and user experience

3. How We Protect Your Information

We are committed to protecting your personal data and have implemented security measures to prevent unauthorized access or misuse of your information. This includes:

  • Data encryption on sensitive information
  • Restricted access to your personal data
  • Regular security audits and updates to our platform

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted partners to provide certain services (e.g., profile verification, customer support) under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Choices

You can control how your information is shared on Matrimonial Hub. You may:

  • Update or edit your profile at any time
  • Deactivate or delete your account if you no longer wish to use our services
  • Contact us to address any concerns or request changes to how your data is used

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at any time.
