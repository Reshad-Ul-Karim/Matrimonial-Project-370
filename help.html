Safety Rules

Your safety is our top priority. Follow these guidelines to ensure a safe and secure experience on Matrimonial Hub.

1. Protect Your Personal Information

Never share sensitive personal details such as your home address, financial information, or passwords with anyone you meet online. Communicate through the platform until you are comfortable.

2. Be Cautious with Requests for Money

Never send money or share your banking details with anyone you meet on the site. Be wary of users who ask for financial assistance for any reason.

3. Report Suspicious Behavior

If you notice any suspicious activity, fraudulent profiles, or inappropriate behavior, please report it to our support team immediately. We will take prompt action.

4. Meet in Public Places

If you decide to meet someone in person, choose a public place for your initial meetings. Inform a trusted friend or family member about your plans and keep them updated.

5. Trust Your Instincts

If something feels off or uncomfortable, trust your instincts and take a step back. You are not obligated to continue conversations or relationships that make you uneasy.

6. Use Verified Profiles

Prefer interacting with users who have verified profiles. Our verification system helps ensure the authenticity of members on our platform.

7. Keep Your Account Secure

Use a strong and unique password for your Matrimonial Hub account. Change it regularly and avoid using the same password on other websites.
